# Metrics

# Pass Rate

## Definition

Percentage of tests that passed.

---

## Formula


Pass Rate =
Passed Tests
/
Executed Tests
× 100


---

## Example


950 Passed
1000 Executed

Pass Rate = 95%


---

# Fail Rate

## Definition

Percentage of tests that failed.

---

## Formula


Fail Rate =
Failed Tests
/
Executed Tests
× 100


---

## Thresholds


< 5% Good

5%-10% Warning

> 10% Critical


---

# Coverage

## Definition

Measures testing completeness.

---

## Types

### Requirement Coverage

Requirements linked to tests.

---

### Test Coverage

Features covered by tests.

---

### Automation Coverage

Automated tests vs total tests.

---

## Formula


Coverage =
Covered Items
/
Total Items
× 100


---

# Defect Density

## Definition

Defects relative to application size.

---

## Formula


Defect Density =
Defects
/
Feature Count


Alternative:


Defects
/
KLOC


---

## Categories

* Critical
* High
* Medium
* Low

---

# MTTR

## Definition

Mean Time To Resolution.

---

## Formula


MTTR =
Total Resolution Time
/
Resolved Defects


---

## Example


200 Hours

/

50 Defects

=

4 Hours


---

# Flakiness

## Definition

Measures test instability.

---

## Formula


Flakiness =
Intermittent Failures
/
Total Runs
× 100


---

## Classification

### Stable


0%-2%


---

### Warning


2%-10%


---

### Critical


>10%
